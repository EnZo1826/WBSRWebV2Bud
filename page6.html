KHULASANDE CAPITAL

Through our 49% interest in the Khulasande Capital Partnership (KCP), a black-owned, controlled and significantly influenced investment vehicle, our group has the ability to invest as a credible and commercial BEE partner. KCP aims to grow South African enterprises while contributing to the broad-based South African society and economy through social initiatives.
