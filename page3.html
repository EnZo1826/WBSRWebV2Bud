Bud INDUSTRIAL SERVICES

Bud Industrial Services was created through the integration of a number of South Africa’s oldest and most established industrial brands including Concord Cranes, Goscor, Uni-span, Prowalco Tatsuno, Augusta Steel and Afrit.

‘Bud Industrial Services provides our entrepreneurs the freedom to operate in a decentralised environment.’

Peter Amm
CEO Bud Industrial Services

UNI-SPAN

Uni-Span is an international supplier and manufacturer of formwork and scaffolding. The company offers formwork and scaffolding solutions to the infrastructure, commercial and petrochemical industries, both nationally and internationally.

GOSCOR

Each company represents premier global brands and provides unique products and services to the industrial sector such as Forklifts & materials Handling, Industrial Cleaning Equipment Solutions and Earthmoving & Compact Equipment.

CONCORD CRANES

Concord Group provides mobile crane hire and rigging services to the industrial, construction, refinery and mining sectors in Southern Africa. Concord provides lifting services with a fleet of in excess of 170 cranes including hydraulic mobile cranes, truck cranes and all terrain cranes with lifting capacity up to 750 tonnes.

PROWALCO TATSUNO

Prowalco Tatsuno manufactures, supplies and maintains fuel pumps and dispensers. Prowalco Tatsuno have the largest pump service infrastructure in Africa which offers market leading manufacturing and supplying of fuel pumps, dispensers and ancillary related products to petroleum companies in South and sub-Saharan Africa.

AUGUSTA STEEL

Augusta Steel manufactures high-quality steel tubing and profiling as well as formwork & scaffolding products. Augusta Steel has a 30 000m² steel processing plant, with the ability to handle heavy coils of rolled steel up to 30 tonnes.

AFRIT

The Afrit Group provides a comprehensive trailer solution to the market including trailer rentals, insurance, short-term financing and second-hand trailer sales services. Afrit manufactures a range of high quality bespoke and innovative trailers including: side-tippers, flat-decks, tautliners, end tippers, refrigeration units, side curtain trailers, oil & gas tankers, car carriers and other specialised trailers used throughout Southern Africa. The Afrit Group is based in Rosslyn with sales and distribution branches across Southern Africa.

AUTOCAST

Autocast is a manufacturer specialising in the supply of aluminium and iron cast machined components predominantly to the local and international automotive industry and grinding media to the mining industry.
